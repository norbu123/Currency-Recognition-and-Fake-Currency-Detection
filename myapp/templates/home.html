Currency Recognition and Fake Currency Detection

This system focuses on identifying and categorizing the Ngultrum, the Bhutanese currency.The list of currencies contains the following denominations: Nu. 1, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100, 500, and 1000
Additionally, it has the ability to recognize fake Indian currency.

Aim

To build an application that can classify Bhutanese currency notes (except old). Along with that we also aim to identify counterfeit Indian notes.

About Project

Welcome to our website which utilizes the power of deep learning technology to classify Bhutanese Ngultrum and also detect
counterfeit Indian banknotes. Our website is designed to provide a simple and efficient solution for anyone who wants to quickly and accurately determine the authenticity of their banknotes. With just a few clicks, you can easily upload an image of your banknote and our system will do the tricks.

The deep learning technology we use is specifically trained to
identify and classify Bhutanese Ngultrum. Paper currency is still needed for the vast majority of financial transactions. We understand that the problem of counterfeit banknotes is a growing concern, and our trained algorithm helps distinguish between genuine and counterfeit Indian banknotes. This means that you can trust our website to provide you with reliable and accurate results.

Step To Get Start

Step 01

(Option 1) Select an option to scan the money using camera
or
(Option 2) Directly upload an image from your device for identification.

Step 02

(Option 1) Scan the currency note with camera

(Option 2) Once you have uploaded the image, wait for some time.

Step 03

(Option 1) The predicted result will be shown on the screen

(Option 2) Click on the predict button.

Step 04

(Option 1) Press "q" to close the camera

(Option 2) The system will predict and verify which note it is.
